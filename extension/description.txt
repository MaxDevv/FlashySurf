FlashySurf V2.1: Study Without Ever Studying

Transform your everyday web browsing into productive study time! Formerly a simple SAT study tool, FlashySurf v2.0 expands into a full study tool where anyone can study anything simply by browsing the web with passive learning flash cards. Users can Now create custom flashcard collections or use our built-in SAT question bank. No more dedicated study sessions, learn while you browse naturally.

HOW IT WORKS:
├── Flashcards appear intelligently while you browse (algorithm adapts to your weak areas)
├── NEW: Create custom flashcard collections for any subject or use built-in SAT questions
├── Answer questions to test your knowledge across multiple subjects
├── Get immediate feedback with detailed explanations
├── Take notes on incorrect answers to reinforce learning
├── Advanced algorithm analyzes mistakes and targets your weaknesses
├── Generate personalized visual performance reports (SAT mode)
└── Learn passively as you continue your normal online activities

KEY FEATURES:
├── Gamified Economy - Earn points for correct answers, spend on breaks, skips, and themes
├── 5 Visual Themes - Dark Mode, Gold, Galaxy (animated stars), Cherry Blossom (falling petals), and Light (free)
├── Referral Program - Earn 75 points instantly when friends install via your referral link
├── Points System - +4 points for correct, +1 for incorrect answers (50 points/day cap), earn unlimited referral bonuses
├── Custom Flashcard Collections - Create, import, and manage flashcards for ANY subject.
├── AI Collections: Users can now convert any document, text or desired topic into flashcard collections with our new AI tool.
├── Collection Management - Import/export collections, enable/disable specific sets
├── Personalized Performance Reports (SAT) - Visual reports identifying your top 3 weakest areas
├── Built-in SAT question bank with Math and English coverage
├── Performance tracking across all active collections with accuracy badges
├── Note-taking system with review capability for incorrect answers
├── Customizable frequency - control how often flashcards appear

├── Detailed explanations for every answer
├── Break timer (20 points) - pause flashcards for 30 minutes when you need focus

FlashySurf uses principles spaced repetition and adaptive learning to help you absorb content gradually. By integrating practice into daily browsing, you build knowledge without dedicated study sessions. Create collections for any subject—languages, history, science, professional certifications, or use our comprehensive SAT question bank. For SAT users it is recommended that you install FlashySurf months in advance before your SAT.

 Advanced Learning Approach
- Semantic Clustering: Questions are grouped into 87 topic clusters using advanced similarity analysis (SAT mode)
- Adaptive Targeting: The system identifies your least accurate topic areas and increases exposure to similar questions
- Performance-Based Selection: Question difficulty and topic selection adapt based on your historical performance
